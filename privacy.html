Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NexGen Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Voluntary Information: We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name or email address if you contact us directly for support or inquiries.

Automated Information: When you visit NexGen Studio, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system via cookies to ensure site stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our Services (PDF editing, text processing).
  • Improve and personalize your document design experience.
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Cookies and Advertising

Google AdSense: As a third-party vendor, Google uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

NexGen Studio is a client-side tool. This means the documents you edit (PDFs, Images) are processed entirely in your browser and are not stored on our servers. Your creative work remains private and secure on your local device.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the developer email provided on the contact page.
