NexGen Studio: Professional User Guide

NexGen Studio is a world-class online editor designed for high-quality document creation. Here is how you can use it to its full potential.

How to Create a PDF?

  • Add text components like Headings and Body from the navbar.
  • Customize fonts and colors using the dynamic floating toolbar.
  • Upload images and use our precision crop tool to fit them into your layout.
  • Once ready, click the 'PDF' button to download your high-resolution file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free? Yes, NexGen Studio is 100% free for everyone.

Are my files safe? Yes, we process everything locally in your browser. No data is stored on our servers.
