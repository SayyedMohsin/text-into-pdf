NexGen Studio User Guide

Learn how to create professional documents with our easy-to-use editor.

How to Create a Professional PDF?

1. Start by adding Heading or Body text from the top navbar. You can double-click any text to edit its content.

2. Use the Floating Toolbar to customize fonts (Inter, Poppins, Montserrat) and colors. You can also adjust text alignment (Left, Center, Right, Justify) for a better layout.

3. Upload Images: Use the upload button to bring in your own logos or photos. Our Precision Crop tool allows you to focus on the perfect area of your image.

4. Managing Pages: Add multiple pages for complex documents and rotate them between Portrait and Landscape using the Rotate tool.
