NexGen Studio: Professional Text to PDF Editor

NexGen Studio is a powerful online editor that helps you create high-quality PDF documents. Whether you need to format text, add professional fonts, or crop images, our tool provides everything on a single dynamic canvas.

Advanced Features:

  • Smart Image Tools: Upload and crop images perfectly using our built-in MediaPipe tracking.
  • Dynamic Layout: Use premium fonts like Poppins and Montserrat to make your documents stand out.
  • Privacy First: Everything is processed in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
